History

Our history

Our long way to world recognition started 25 years ago.

1996
Jane Clarberg and Jeff Mutson found Clarberg & Mutson at 12 Bleecker Street St, New York, NY.
1998
The main focus of the firm's practice is commercial and residential real estate issues.
1999
The firm increases its focus on litigation.
2003
The company expands and moves into the new office in Chicago.
2011
Clarberg & Mutson is ranked as the top plaintiff-side class action firm in the USA by Cooper & Partners, London, UK.
